About VIA Club

History

They are Earth's mightiest heroes, formed to fight the foes no single hero could withstand. The Avengers VIA Club are the most prestigious and powerful super-hero club in the world, an evershifting assemblage of super-beings, adventurers and crimefighters devoted to protecting the planet from menaces beyond the scope of conventional authorities and rivaling football clubs. The group began with the random teaming of Thor, Iron Man, Ant-Man (Hank Pym), Wasp and Hulk, who joined forces to thwart the Asgardian menace Loki in response to a call for help from Hulk's teen sidekick, Rick Jones. Pym suggested the heroes remain together as a team, and his partner Wasp suggested they call themselves "something colorful and dramatic, like ...the Avengers." The name stuck. Coach Oldenuff suggested a mere human name for everyday use. A legend club was born. VIA CLUB.
